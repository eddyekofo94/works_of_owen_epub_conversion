<p class="title-line title-line-major">A VINDICATION</p>
<p class="title-line title-line-medium">OF SOME PASSAGES</p>
<p class="title-line title-line-medium">IN A</p>
<p class="title-line title-line-medium">DISCOURSE CONCERNING</p>
<p class="title-line title-line-medium">COMMUNION WITH GOD</p>
<p class="descriptive">It is now near twenty years since I wrote and published a Discourse</p>
<p class="descriptive">concerning Communion with God. Of what use and advantage it has been</p>
<p class="descriptive">to any, as to their furtherance in the design aimed at therein, is left unto</p>
<p class="descriptive">them to judge by whom it has been perused with any candid diligence; and</p>
<p class="descriptive">I do know that multitudes of persons fearing God, and desiring to walk</p>
<p class="descriptive">before him in sincerity, are ready, if occasion require, to give testimony</p>
<p class="descriptive">unto the benefit which they have received thereby; — as I can also at any</p>
<p class="descriptive">time produce the testimonies of [as] learned and holy persons, it may be,</p>
<p class="descriptive">as any I know living, both in England and out of it, who, owning the truth</p>
<p class="descriptive">contained in it, have highly avowed its usefulness, and are ready yet so to</p>
<p class="descriptive">do. With all other persons, so far as ever I heard, it passed at the rate of a</p>
<p class="descriptive">tolerable acceptation with discourses of the same kind and nature. And</p>
<p class="descriptive">however any thing or passage in it might not, possibly, suit the</p>
<p class="descriptive">apprehensions of some, yet, being wholly practical, designed for popular</p>
<p class="descriptive">edification, without any direct engagement into things controversial, I</p>
<p class="descriptive">looked for no opposition unto it or exception against it; but that it would</p>
<p class="descriptive">at least be suffered to pass at that rate of allowance which is universally</p>
<p class="descriptive">granted unto that sort of writings, both of ancient and modern authors.</p>
<div class="quote-block">Accordingly it so fell out, and continued for many years; until some persons began to judge it their interest, and to make it their business, to cavil at my writings, and to load my person with reproaches.
