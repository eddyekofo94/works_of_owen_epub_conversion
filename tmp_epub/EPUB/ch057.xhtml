CHAPTER 9

OF THE INCARNATION OF CHRIST.

Q. 1. Shall all mankind, then, everlastingly perish?

A. No; God, of his free grace, has prepared a way to redeem and save his elect. John 3:16; Isaiah 53:6.

Q. 2. What way was this?

A. By sending his own Son61 Jesus Christ in the likeness of sinful flesh, condemning sin Romans 8:3.

Q. 3. Who is this you call his own Son?

A. The second person of the Trinity, coeternal and of the one Deity with his Father. John 1:14; Romans 1:3; Galatians 4:4; 1 John 1:1.

Q. 4. How did God send him?

A. By causing him to be made flesh of a pure virgin, and to dwell among us, that he might be obedient unto death, the death of the cross. Isaiah 50:6; John 1:14; Luke 1:35; Philippians 23:8; 1 Timothy 3:16.
